About Us

We are a group of people with decades of experience in growing and selling oranges. We know what a good orange looks like and how they are grown. This essential fruit requires a temperate climate, loamy soil that is rich in organic matter apart from needing proper drainage and aeration facilities. Lucky for us we live in Kashmir which is proudly known as paradise of earth is also home to temperate fruits like orange for which the state is very famous across globe. The main factor which influence temperate fruit bearing trees is soil, climate and environment which are highly favorable and unparalleled in the province of Kashmir.

Varieties

Mandarin Orange

Mandarin oranges are the smallest type of orange. They have a soft skin that's easy to peel and are virtually seedless

Navel Orange

These oranges are sweet and seedless, which makes them the perfect snacking orange to eat out of hand.



Tangerines

Tangerines are small, brightly colored oranges. They have slightly looser peels than most oranges, which makes them easy to peel and eat out of hand.

Clementines

They're round, almost entirely seedless, and have a skin that is tight, but thin and easy to peel.
